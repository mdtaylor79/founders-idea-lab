The basics

What is an entrepreneur?

A person who finds a problem people care about and builds a solution they value.

You do not need age, money, or permission. You need a problem worth solving.

The first move

Start with a problem, not a product

1
Notice
Watch for the small frustrations people put up with every day.
2
Ask who hurts
Name the person who feels the problem most.
3
Then build
Shape the simplest thing that helps that person.
The tool

What is AI?

A tool that helps you think, make, and build faster.

AI refines. It does not replace. You are still the founder.

Leverage

How founders use AI

  • Find opportunities and spot real problems
  • Research a market in minutes, not weeks
  • Draft ideas, names, and designs
  • Get fast, honest feedback on a plan
  • Sharpen a pitch before facing an investor

The business part

A real venture makes money

Subscription
People pay every month to keep using it.
A study app for a few dollars a month.
Freemium
Free to start, pay for the extras.
A free game that sells new skins.
Marketplace fee
Connect buyers and sellers, take a small cut.
A resale app that keeps a slice of each sale.
One-time sale
Pay once to own it.
A product or an app you buy outright.
Working with AI

Talk to AI like a founder

Role
Tell the AI who to be.
Act like a startup coach for teens.
Goal
Say what you want.
Give me 3 app ideas for better sleep.
Details
Add who and where.
For high school athletes who stay up late.
Format
Say how to show it.
List each with a name and one line.
